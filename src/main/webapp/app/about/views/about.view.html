How it works

Step 1: Register for the Trial Direct service

Step 2: Use our innovative search engine to find suitable trials for your patient in your geographic location

Step 3: Learn more about the trial and the physicians running it

Step 4: If the trial is a viable option, your patient can register for the trial through Trial Direct

Step 5: The physician running the trial will contact your patient to discuss further

You can also receive, at your discretion, a small administration fee of $25 for the time taken to consider the trial for your patient. This payment is paid direct to your bank account in 14 days.
