How it works

  • 1 Register for the Trial Direct service
  • 2 Use our innovative search engine to find suitable trials for your patient in your geographic location
  • 3 Learn more about the trial and the physicians running it
  • 4 If the trial is a viable option, your patient can register for the trial through Trial Direct
  • 5 The physician running the trial will contact your patient to discuss further

You can also receive, at your discretion, a small administration fee of $25 for the time taken to consider the trial for your patient. This payment is paid direct to your bank account in 14 days.

About Trial Direct

Trial Direct empowers physicians to consider clinical trials as a treatment option for their patients. Your patients want to know about clinical trials so they can consider all treatment options. Your patients PREFER to learn about trials from their PHYSICIAN.

Like any other treatment option, you need the RIGHT INFORMATION to consider a clinical trial for your patient. Trial Direct provides INSTANT ACCESS to the information you need.

You can take the DECISION with your patient to contact a physician running a trial. Trial Direct, putting the power and access of TOMORROW'S medicines and treatment options in the hands of doctors and their patients TODAY.
